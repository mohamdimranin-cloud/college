Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What are the eligibility criteria for admission?

+

Students who have passed 10th standard (SSLC) from any recognized board are eligible to apply. Minimum 35% marks required for general category and 30% for reserved categories.

What is the fee structure?

+

Annual fees range from ‚Çπ25,000 to ‚Çπ35,000 depending on the stream. This includes tuition, lab fees, library, and sports. Scholarships available for meritorious students.

Do you provide hostel facilities?

+

We have tie-ups with nearby hostels that provide safe and comfortable accommodation for outstation students. Both boys and girls hostels available with mess facilities.

Is coaching for competitive exams included?

+

Yes! We provide integrated coaching for NEET, JEE, and CET as part of the curriculum. Regular mock tests, study materials, and doubt-clearing sessions are conducted.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

+

We maintain a healthy student-teacher ratio of 30:1 to ensure personalized attention. Each class has maximum 30 students for effective learning.

Are there any extracurricular activities?

+

Absolutely! We organize sports tournaments, cultural events, science exhibitions, debates, quiz competitions, and educational tours throughout the year for holistic development.
